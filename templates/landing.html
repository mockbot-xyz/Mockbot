How It Works

From signup to hilarious automated messages in 5 simple steps

1

Sign Up with Twitch

Click "Get Started Free" and authorize with your Twitch account. We'll automatically detect your channel name and set up your bot profile. No email, no passwords, no hassle.

2

Configure Your Bot

Complete the quick onboarding to set your preferences: message frequency, reply style, and whether the bot should auto-post or wait for commands. You can change these anytime.

3

Bot Joins Your Channel

ANSV bot automatically joins your Twitch channel and starts monitoring chat. It stays connected 24/7 (even when you're offline) to continuously learn from your community.

4

AI Learns From Your Chat

As your community chats, the Markov chain AI builds a model of their language patterns, emotes, inside jokes, and common phrases. The more messages, the better it gets!

5

Bot Generates Hilarious Messages

The bot automatically posts messages that sound like they came from your chat. It mimics your community's style perfectly - including broken grammar, random emotes, and inside jokes. Optionally upgrade to Premium for janky TTS voices!
